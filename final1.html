Investor Awareness: Beware of Unauthorised Investment Promises from Individuals Claiming Association with EPM Wealth

We have come across reports of individuals and unauthorised groups on WhatsApp and other social media platforms falsely claiming association with EPM Wealth, its senior leadership and brand identity — offering investment opportunities with intent to mislead and defraud individuals. EPM Wealth is not associated with these individuals or unauthorised groups. Investors are advised to exercise extreme caution and independently verify the authenticity of such individuals before engaging in any transaction.

Please Click here to view a list of identified fraud accounts.

Please note:

EPM Wealth does NOT approach investors through unsolicited phone calls, messages, emails, or social media platforms for investments.

EPM Wealth does NOT offer or assure guaranteed returns for any investment whatsoever.

For investments, payments are made only to designated accounts bearing the name of the fund and scheme — not to any unrelated accounts.

Investors are encouraged to verify all investment-related communications through official channels, including our website at www.epmwealth.com

If you receive any such unauthorized communication, please inform us at info@epmwealth.com or report it with local authorities and AMFI/SEBI.
